### Key Findings

**Production Context**:
- Scribe Ange Vergèce was Cretan Greek, royal scribe under Francis I
- His handwriting became basis for Garamond's *Grecs du Roi* typeface
- Copied during Pellicier's embassy to Venice (1539-42)
- MS predates all printed editions of its texts

**Textual Significance**:
